Part 2: The Intuition

Return Distributions Instead of Single Numbers

Think of it as: Instead of saying "the stock will go up 12%," we say: "in this regime there is 65% chance of +5% to +35%, 20% chance of flat, and 15% chance of -10% to -30%."

This gives you the full picture of risk and reward.

Regime Conditioning = Contextual Intelligence

Think of it as: The weather forecast changes completely depending on whether it's summer or winter. The same applies to stock returns in trend vs. high-vol regimes.

Conditioning on regime dramatically improves forecast realism.
